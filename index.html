"south Indian lunch"

South Indian lunch: a medley of flavors and traditions. Revel in the aromatic blend of spices as you savor dishes like rice, sambar, rasam, curd with porial, kootu, varuval, appalam, flavorful curries, and tangy tamarind rice, vadai & pickle.This is a tasty & filling menu during occasions, festivals and day to day cooking.
